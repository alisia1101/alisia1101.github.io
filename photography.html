Information

Jose Fonseca, Darryl Sanders, Alisia Neumen, Garibaldo Trujilio, Jasmine Aranda and Treyvon Creep Fest Charcoal, paper 10/19/2015

Hello this our piece, Creep Fest. As you may be able to tell this selection is supposed to create a sense of “fear” or “creepiness”. Our original idea had heavy inspirations from Japanese and American style horror having direct roots to “The Grudge” and “The Ring” while bringing the goriness from movies like “Friday the Thirteenth” and “Nightmare on Elm Street”. Now as you can see we fell closer to “The Ring” and little to no gore. First we began by drawing horror images in charcoal and edited the photos into a short film, along with adding a haunting audio soundtrack. We want to make it look as creepy as possible. After we filmed a sort of homage to several of our inspirations and inserted them into our video. In the end we are nervous but hopeful that our installation will bring out the emotions that we are looking for.
